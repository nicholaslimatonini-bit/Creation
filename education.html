Education

Springwood High School

I attended Springwood High School and went there for all years (Year 7 to Year 11), I did plenty of stuff there, including GCSEs and some extra-curriculars.

For extra-curriculars, I did stuff like sing in a choir, play guitar in a school orchestra band, after-school astronomy, and D&T.

College of West Anglia

After finishing Year 11 in high school, I went to the College of West Anglia and did a course on Level 2 in Computer Science. We've done three topics on that course. In which in two of those topics, that were on Web Development and Digital Portfolio (the one you're reading right now), we've done a five assignments. However, for Online World, we did one written exam for that.

For the web development topic, we did three assignments;

  • One was about researching two websites and describing it's fundamental features, target audience, and advantages and disadvantages.
  • The second one, was on creating wireframes, preparing assets for a website we were about to develop, and talking about alternative solutions, aesthetic features, use of interactive features, etc.
  • The third and final one on that topic was to create a functional and responsive website based on the aesthetics, designs, and the target audience I've discussed last time. after the web development was finished, I had to provide screenshots of the webpages that have been created as well as saving the actual webpages on specific folders too.

I was impressed with my overall work I've put into it and I hope to make these amazing websites again in the near future.
