For extra-curriculars, I did stuff like sing in a choir, play guitar in a school orchestra band, after-school astronomy, and D&T.

College of West Anglia

After finishing Year 11 in high school, I went to the College of West Anglia and did a course on Level 2 in Computer Science.

We did five assignments in total on two topics, and did a written exam on Online World. So, three of those assigments I did were on web development, one was about researching two websites and describing it's fundamental features, target audience, and advantages and disadvantages. The other one, was on creating wireframes and preparing assets for a website we were about to develop, and I've talked about alternative solutions, aesthetic features, use of interactive features, etc. The third and final one on that topic was to create a functional and responsive website based on the aesthetics, designs, and the target audience we've talked about last time. Then, after the web development was finished, we had to provide screenshots of the webpages we've created as well as attaching the actual webpages too. I was impressed overall with the work I've put into them and I hope to make these amazing websites again in the near future.
