Early Life

I was born on the 17th of July 2009 at the Chelsea and Westminster Hospital in London, England to Brazilian parents, I was the first of the two children my parents have including my brother, who was born three years later. On my father's side, I am of Italian and Austrian descent, I am a descendant of immigrants from Ancona (in the Marche Region), in Italy, and on my mother's side I have primarily Italian ancestry.

I lived most of my childhood here in the UK (particularly Greater London), but when I was two years old, I moved to Brazil, because my mum was pregnant with my brother and needed her family to look after and take care of her. When my brother was two years old and I was five. My mum moved back to the UK to look for work, and left us there with our aunts, grandparents, and cousins. My father also stayed as well to work as a car mechanic working with and selling cars at a garage near our house. During that time, I attended nursery and started going to school, where I developed communication, teamwork, and problem solving skills.

When I was seven years old (in Late December 2016), my mum has come back to Brazil to pick me and brother up and take us back to the UK after four years. Since we came back, we lived in London for a year and a half. So firstly, our parents left us with our cousins at their house in Dollis Hill and stayed with them for a couple of months, and then our parents picked us up from their house and we moved in with them at their house, in Richmond, where we were finally family. At that time, I attended Marshgate C of E Primary School in Richmond.
